Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always check website URLs before entering details
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Never share OTPs with anyone
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking transactions
- Regularly monitor your bank account activity
- Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline 1930 in case of fraud
